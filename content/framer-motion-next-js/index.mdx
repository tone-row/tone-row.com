title: Awful page transitions with Framer Motion and Next.js
published: 2021-05-19

For years I've wanted express control over page transitions in websites and apps. With [framer-motion](https://www.framer.com/motion/) and [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/) it feels like we finally have a great pair to make a lot of animations within reach.

There are good tutorials on the basic setup by xxxx and xxxx that got me exploring. This post will focus on some setup, and some gotchas to help you plan for adding this to your site.
